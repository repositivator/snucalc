Introduction to Application

In oncology trial,
the important outcomes for treatment effect are response rate and progression free survival
which are evaluated based on RECIST (Response Evaluation Criteria In Solid Tumors)
This application calculates the distribution of proportion of patients
who were diagnosed with partial response (or progression)
when same radiologist or other radiologists re-measure all CT scans.
