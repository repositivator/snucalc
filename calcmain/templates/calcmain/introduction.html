Introduction to Application

In recent oncology trials,
surrogate endpoints represented by progression-free survival and objective response rate
have gradually taken the primary role. These endpoints are imaging-based outcomes assessed
by RECIST (Response Evaluation Criteria In Solid Tumors) response categorization
according to the percent change of tumor burden before and after treatment.
The radiologic measurements of baseline and post-treatment tumor burden may be inconsistent between readings. This application estimate how the objective response rate would vary due to the measurement variability when same or another radiologist re-assesses tumor response.
